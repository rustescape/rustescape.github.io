About Us

"ANTIKORS BALTIC" SIA Our specialization is development, manufacture and realization of anti-corrosion treatments for metal, concrete, wood. In the base of manufacture of our products were taken formulas of the products developed and placed into production in the experimental engineering department of “Antikors” at the Institute of Non-Organic Chemistry in year of 1986. Based on the experience of “Antikors”, SIA “Antikors” and other manufacturers working in this industry, considering the progress in the technology and manufacture of the raw materials for the past 30 years, we worked up and improved those recipes. Only quality raw materials from leading European fabricators are used in the manufacture of our products. ​
